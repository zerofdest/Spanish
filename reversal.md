## 间接宾语代词 + 动词 + 主语

常见动词（高频）：

* **gustar** – to like
* **doler** – to hurt / to cause pain
* **interesar** – to interest
* **molestar** – to bother / to annoy
* **faltar** – to lack / to be missing
* **quedar** – to remain / to suit / to fit
* **encantar** – to love / to be delighted by
* **preocupar** – to worry / to concern
* **importar** – to matter / to care about
* **parecer** – to seem / to appear (to someone)


例如：

* **Me interesa la lingüística.**
* **Le molestan los ruidos.**
* **Nos faltan dos sillas.**
* **Te queda bien esa camisa.**
